🔥 The Fire: Why We Keep Going

Hate spreads fast. But fire spreads faster.

The Spark lit the match — but The Fire is what keeps it burning. It’s the fuel that turns one brave voice into a movement.

Every time someone chooses to ✊ Empower, 🔄 Flip, or 💡 Leave Light, the flame grows brighter. Trolls feed on silence. We feed on courage.

Fire is disruptive. It demands attention. But this isn’t destruction — it’s illumination. It’s showing the truth in spaces where lies spread unchecked.

That’s why The Fire matters. It’s not just one post, one comment, or one drop. It’s thousands of sparks combining — until hate can’t drown out the glow.

This is The Fire. 🔥 It’s not just a reaction — it’s a revolution of empathy.

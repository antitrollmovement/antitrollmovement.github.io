📖 The EFL Playbook

Empower · Flip · Light

The EFL Playbook is at the heart of the AntiTrollMovement. It’s not about fighting trolls — it’s about creating a ripple effect of change. Simple actions anyone can take to leave something brighter behind. 💡

✊ Empower

Don’t feed the troll — empower the target. Show them they’re not alone. Your words can be the shield they need.

🔄 Flip

Flip the script. Take the energy of hate and redirect it into truth, humour, or perspective. Trolls lose power when their words don’t land.

💡 Light

Always leave light. A kind word, a positive spin, or even just a 💡 emoji. It shows the troll’s darkness doesn’t get the final word.
